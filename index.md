          <h1 class="t__h1">Update presence / absence for a face-to-face session</h1>
          <div class="article__desc">
            How do I update my present / absent learners in a face-to-face session ?
          </div>
          <div class="avatar">
  <div class="avatar__photo o__ltr">
      <img src="https://static.intercomassets.com/avatars/4004378/square_128/photo_profil-1587972480.png?1587972480" alt="Claire Philippe avatar" class="avatar__image">

  </div>
  <div class="avatar__info">
    <div>
      Written by <span class="c__darker"> Claire Philippe</span>
        <br> Updated over a week ago
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

      </div>
      <article dir="ltr"><p class="no-margin">In order to update the presence and absence of your learners, you will first need to create your face-to-face sessions, to have registered your learners there.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">To learn more about creating and managing face-to-face sessions, see <a href="https://help.riseup.ai/fr/articles/3975362">Managing face-to-face sessions</a>.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin"></p><h2 id="access" data-post-processed="true">Access</h2><p class="no-margin">Access: <b>Side Menu &gt; Training &gt; Manage face-to-face sessions</b></p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">By clicking on <b>More options </b>(three vertical dots) for the desired session, you can then click on <b>View users </b>to access the list of your registrants. </p><p class="no-margin"></p><div class="intercom-container"><a href="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268510705/9309109358b22063d1232be6/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=e69a239547cda4fe3033b25209c1d5436a0a4083a3e2609f1e1ffb71a50a7080" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268510705/9309109358b22063d1232be6/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=e69a239547cda4fe3033b25209c1d5436a0a4083a3e2609f1e1ffb71a50a7080"></a></div><p class="no-margin">A pop-up window showing the list of registrants to date will appear.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><div class="intercom-container"><a href="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268510909/cfed264124ecabf20c91c2b2/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=ed11a9c3dc6e7bf3a7999126c9b69d7a9c0b7939ffb18303f3f180b12540a283" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268510909/cfed264124ecabf20c91c2b2/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=ed11a9c3dc6e7bf3a7999126c9b69d7a9c0b7939ffb18303f3f180b12540a283"></a></div><p class="no-margin">You will find the following information there:</p><ul><li><p class="no-margin">First and last name of the learner</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Session registration date</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Registration status</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Duration of the session (or presence)</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Presence</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Details&gt; View, will allow you to access the learner's detailed progress</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Documents&gt; Notice of meeting and Attestation of attendance (see details below).</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Actions&gt; by clicking on the cross icon ‚ùå, the learner is unsubscribed from the session</p></li></ul><h2 id="updating-learners-attendance" data-post-processed="true">Updating learners' attendance</h2><p class="no-margin">To update your learners' attendance / absence of your learners, access the drop-down menu in the <b>Attendance </b>column. By default, your learners will have the status "<b>Not set</b>", and you can then carry out the update by indicating <b>Here</b> or <b>Note here</b>.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><div class="intercom-container"><a href="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268511749/dc711fbcd85b0555a01d8c65/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=1850469c24f2aaf8ee4e0bd8df303de86756151e23bcb4e46d4c88d79e956f67" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268511749/dc711fbcd85b0555a01d8c65/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=1850469c24f2aaf8ee4e0bd8df303de86756151e23bcb4e46d4c88d79e956f67"></a></div><p class="no-margin">Once the Attendance have been updated, click on <b>Validate</b>.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">As soon as you click on Validate, the corresponding <b>attendance certificates </b>will be automatically generated, and depending on the settings of your training, will also be sent to your learners.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">As a reminder, you can decide whether or not to <b>send Legal Documents </b>to learners in the <a href="https://help.riseup.ai/fr/articles/3974276">Configuration section of the creation of your training courses</a>.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">You can also click on <b>Everyone present</b>, to automatically indicate that all of your learners have attended the session. The certificates will then be generated / sent.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin">‚ö†Ô∏è By clicking on <b>Everyone present</b>, the entry is automatically <b>Validate </b>and save. It is therefore not recommended to click on <b>Everyone present</b>, to then update only the absences, because a certificate of attendance will then be generated for the learners indicated absent afterwards.</p><p class="no-margin"></p><h2 id="documents-generated" data-post-processed="true"><br>Documents generated</h2><p class="no-margin">The documents generated are:</p><ul><li><p class="no-margin"><b>Invitation to a classroom</b> (<b>Convocation</b>) &gt; The document is created when registering for the session.</p></li><li><p class="no-margin"><b>Attestation of attendance </b>&gt; The document is created when the learner is indicated Present at the session (see above).</p></li></ul><p class="no-margin">‚ö†Ô∏è Note: It is not possible to update your learners' attendance until the session has started, or has finished.</p><p class="no-margin">Indeed, it is not possible to note that a person has been here to a session that has not yet taken place.</p><p class="no-margin">The interface that will appear will therefore be simplified like this:</p><p class="no-margin"></p><div class="intercom-container"><a href="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268513313/9589da0e94d3998b25badadb/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=804bf9bbdef1ac7565d23ecc95cd7c8f0e31f674047ccf8aa422a02908b7a2b8" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/268513313/9589da0e94d3998b25badadb/image.png?expires=1619959067&amp;signature=804bf9bbdef1ac7565d23ecc95cd7c8f0e31f674047ccf8aa422a02908b7a2b8"></a></div><p class="no-margin"></p><p class="no-margin"><i>Last updated on 11/20/2020.</i></p></article>
